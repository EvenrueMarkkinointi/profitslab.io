## 3. Content Pillars

ProfitSlab's content strategy is built on **5 content pillars** that map directly to the audit categories and user pain points.

### Pillar 1: Content & Marketing
**Core theme:** Creating content faster without sacrificing quality.
- How to create a week's worth of social media in 30 minutes
- Content repurposing: one blog post → 10 pieces of content
- AI prompts for writing newsletters that get opened
- SEO content with AI: ranking without hiring an agency
- Social media content calendar with ChatGPT

### Pillar 2: Sales & Outreach
**Core theme:** Getting more replies and closing more deals.
- Cold email prompts that get 40%+ open rates
- LinkedIn outreach with AI: templates that don't feel spammy
- Sales follow-up sequences: the 5-email framework
- Handling objections with AI: 10 common replies
- Proposal writing with AI: from blank page to sent in 20 minutes

### Pillar 3: Customer Service & Communication
**Core theme:** Faster responses, happier customers, fewer escalations.
- AI customer service: handling complaints without sounding robotic
- Building a customer response template library
- Automating FAQ responses with AI
- Email tone calibration: professional vs. friendly
- De-escalation prompts for angry customers

### Pillar 4: Operations & Admin
**Core theme:** Systematizing the messy stuff that eats your day.
- Writing SOPs with AI: from chaos to documented in one afternoon
- Meeting notes to action items: the 2-minute workflow
- Onboarding new team members with AI-generated checklists
- Invoice and contract drafting with AI
- Internal documentation that people actually read

### Pillar 5: Strategy & Planning
**Core theme:** Making better decisions with less data anxiety.
- Quarterly planning with AI: structured goals in 45 minutes
- Competitor analysis with ChatGPT
- Financial forecasting for non-accountants
- Business idea validation with AI
- Risk assessment: asking AI the right questions

| Add FAQ schema | Missing | FAQPage schema | /faq, /guides/* |
| Add Article schema | Missing | Article schema | All guides |
| Image alt text | Missing | Descriptive alt | All pages |

### 5.2 Content Page SEO Template

Every guide/article should follow this structure:

```
URL: /guides/[slug]
Title: [Benefit-driven title] | ProfitSlab
Meta Description: [What they learn] — step-by-step guide for small business owners. [CTA].
H1: [Primary keyword]
H2: [Secondary keywords]
H3: [Long-tail variations]
Schema: Article + BreadcrumbList + FAQPage
Internal Links: 3–5 to other guides + 1 to audit + 1 to product
CTA: Contextual CTA block (audit or product)
```

### 5.3 Content CTA Blocks

Every guide gets 2 CTA insertions:

**Mid-content CTA (after first H2):**
```

Take the free 5-minute AI Audit → [link to /audit]
```

```
🛠️ Ready to execute? The AI Shortcut Stack has 80 copy-paste prompts 
for [topic]. $19, one-time.
[See the Prompts →] [link to /products/prompt-stack]
```

| Task | Target |
